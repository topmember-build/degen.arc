⚙️ How CCTP Works
Bridge Flow — 4 Steps (~30 seconds total)
1
Approve USDC
Allow Circle's TokenMessenger contract to spend your USDC on the source chain
2
Burn USDC on Source
depositForBurn() destroys USDC on source chain and emits a MessageSent event with your burn hash
3
Circle Attestation (~15-20s)
Circle's Iris API signs the burn message. The dapp polls until the attestation is ready
4
Mint USDC on Arc
receiveMessage() submits the attestation to Arc's MessageTransmitter — native USDC minted to your wallet
